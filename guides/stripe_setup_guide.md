# How to Set Up Your Stripe Account

Welcome! This guide will walk you through creating and verifying your Stripe account so you can start accepting payments. We’ll use simple steps and diagrams to make it easy, even if you’re new to online payments.

---

## 1. Go to the Stripe Website

- Open your browser and go to [https://dashboard.stripe.com/register](https://dashboard.stripe.com/register)

![Step 1: Stripe Registration Page](https://stripe.com/img/v3/home/social.png)

---

## 2. Fill Out the Registration Form

- Enter your email address, full name, and create a strong password.
- Click **Create account**.

![Step 2: Registration Form](https://i.imgur.com/6Qw1QwB.png)

---

## 3. Verify Your Email

- Check your email inbox for a message from Stripe.
- Click the verification link in the email.

---

## 4. Set Up Your Business Details

- Log in to your new Stripe account.
- Click **Start** to begin setting up your business profile.
- Enter your business name, address, and phone number.
- Select your business type (individual, company, etc.).

![Step 4: Business Details](https://i.imgur.com/8Qw1QwB.png)

---

## 5. Add Bank Account

- Go to **Settings > Bank accounts and scheduling**.
- Click **Add bank account**.
- Enter your bank details (account number, routing number, etc.).

---

## 6. Complete Identity Verification

- Stripe will ask for identity documents (like a driver’s license or passport).
- Upload the required documents.

---

## 7. Activate Your Account

- Review all information and submit.
- Stripe will review your details and notify you when your account is active.

---

## 8. Start Accepting Payments

- Once approved, you can create payment links, invoices, or integrate Stripe with your website/app.

---

## Need Help?

- Visit [Stripe Support](https://support.stripe.com/) for more help.
- Or contact our support team from your dashboard.

---

**Congratulations!** You’re ready to accept payments with Stripe.
