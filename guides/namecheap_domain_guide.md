# How to Register and Host Your Domain with Namecheap (and Why It Matters)

This guide will help you register a domain with Namecheap, connect it to your account using our API, and explain why this gives you more control. It’s written for beginners, with clear steps and diagrams.

---

## 1. Why Host Your Own Domain?

- **Full Control:** You own your domain and can change settings anytime.
- **Better Security:** You decide who can access or transfer your domain.
- **Easy Integration:** Connect your domain to any website or service.
- **Earn Rewards:** If you register through our link, we may receive a small commission (at no extra cost to you).

---

## 2. Register a Domain on Namecheap

- Go to [https://www.namecheap.com/](https://www.namecheap.com/)
- Use the search bar to find your desired domain name.
- Click **Add to Cart** and then **Checkout**.
- Create a Namecheap account if you don’t have one.
- Complete the purchase.

![Step 2: Namecheap Search](https://i.imgur.com/1Qw1QwB.png)

---

## 3. Access Your Domain Dashboard

- Log in to Namecheap.
- Go to **Domain List** from the left menu.
- Click **Manage** next to your new domain.

![Step 3: Domain Dashboard](https://i.imgur.com/2Qw1QwB.png)

---

## 4. Connect Your Domain Using Our API

- In your dashboard, look for **Advanced DNS**.
- Add a new record:
  - **Type:** CNAME or A (we’ll tell you which one)
  - **Host:** The subdomain you want (e.g., `www` or `app`)
  - **Value:** The address we provide (e.g., your app’s URL or IP)
- Save changes.

*If you’re using our platform, you can automate this step from your dashboard!*

---

## 5. Wait for DNS Propagation

- Changes can take up to 24 hours, but usually much faster.
- You can check your domain status at [https://dnschecker.org/](https://dnschecker.org/)

---

## 6. Why This Is the Best Way

- You keep ownership and control.
- You can move your website or email anywhere, anytime.
- You’re not locked into one provider.
- You help support us by using our referral link!

---

## 7. Need Help?

- Namecheap Support: [https://www.namecheap.com/support/](https://www.namecheap.com/support/)
- Or contact us from your dashboard for step-by-step help.

---

**Congratulations!** You now have your own domain, fully under your control.
